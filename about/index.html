I'm a data science enthusiast. My interests include deep learning, machine learning, data analysis and programming in Python.

I previously worked as Senior Researcher at Strathclyde University, UK and as an Industrial Associate at Building Research Establishment, UK and received a PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Strathclyde University, UK.

Software

  • ESP-r: Advanced energy systems performance simulation (Fortran back-end, C/C++ front-end, SQL/XML/JSON/csv outputs )
  • HUE: Big data powered policy tool for upgrading houses based on models for the entire UK housing stock
